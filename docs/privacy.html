Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

At JustRun, available from the Google Play Store, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by JustRun and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

JustRun is designed to be a minimalist tool. To provide its core functionality—tracking your runs—we collect the following data:

  • Location Data: We use your device's GPS to calculate distance, pace, and route in real-time.
  • Activity Data: Duration of your runs, timestamps, and calculated statistics are stored to provide you with a history of your activities.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our app.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand tracking accuracy.
  • Maintain a history of your fitness activities.
  • Manage notifications for active runs.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is paramount. Most data collected by JustRun is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your coordinates to any central server unless you explicitly enable cloud synchronization features (e.g., via Google Login).

4. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Location: Required for real-time distance tracking.
  • Notifications: Required to show active run statistics while the app is in the background.

5. Service Providers

We may use third-party services such as Google Play Services to facilitate app updates and monitor performance. These third parties have access to your Personal Information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
